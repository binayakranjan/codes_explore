Requirements of the system:

A)Functional Requirements::
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++
1. Newsfeed will be generated based on the people, pages & groups the user
follows.
2. Embedded items may be images, videos or text.
3. Feeds can be from one or many people, pages & groups.
4. Feeds can be appended as when active users post.

B)Non-functional Requirements::
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
1. Latency between an active user's post and newsfeed appearance should be as
minimum as possible eg 5-7 secs.
2. Latency between the user's newsfeed request and rendering should be as
minimum as possible eg 1-2 secs.
